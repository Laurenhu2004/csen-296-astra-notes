Week 2.1 Lab: Working Agreement and Definition of Done for AstraNotes

Assignment Instructions
For this lab, I want you to take the Agile and Scrum ideas from class and turn them into something operational for your AstraNotes project.

This is not a theory summary. I do not want a generic explanation of Agile, Scrum, or the Spotify model. I want you to define how your AstraNotes work will actually be governed over the quarter.

For this assignment, you will create two connected artifacts:

Part 1 - a Working Agreement
Part  2- a Definition of Done
Even if you are working individually, I still want you to approach this like a professional software workflow. That means you need a clear way to manage AI use, decision-making, quality expectations, and project discipline.

Part 2: Definition of Done
Your Definition of Done should explain what must be true before a task, feature, or artifact is considered complete.

Your Definition of Done should address ideas such as:

whether the work maps to a real objective or requirement
whether you can explain the logic or design
whether quality and realism have been checked
whether testing, traceability, or validation has been considered
whether security, privacy, or governance concerns have been ignored
whether the work deserves to move forward
What I Want You To Try on AstraNotes
As you create these two artifacts, I want you to test them against your real project.

Ask yourself:

would this Definition of Done accept my current AI-generated work yet?
what gap in my workflow would create confusion later if I do not fix it now?
